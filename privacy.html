Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Welcome to yuvi31.in. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and web tools.

1. Data Processing for Web Tools

The tools provided on this website, including the photo mosaic generator and expense tracker, are designed with your privacy in mind.

  • Images: Photos uploaded to the photo mosaic generator are processed locally in your web browser. We do not upload, save, or store your images on our servers.

2. Third-Party Ads and Cookies

We use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Log Files

Like most standard websites, we utilize log files. These files log visitors to the site to track metrics like browser type, referring/exit pages, and click counts. This information is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
